How Titles Work

Titles give a few benefits to players, there are 3 levels of titles. On ascending order there are Basic, Profession, and Advanced titles. to get the title in a higher level, you must first get two titles of a lower level that Leads to the title.

Apenedix of Titles

Basic

Pilgrim Vagabond Scholar burglar Oblate

Profession

Deacon Vicar Adventurer Knight

Advanced
