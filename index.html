The Ascian Tower

Welcome Climber

The Tower Itself

A seemingly endless stone complex accends from the depths of the crystal sea. Its exeterior is covered in stairs and bridges, cliffs and out crops, runes and warnings. No one knows when the tower apeared, no one knows why the tower was made, and no one knows whats at the top. If the tower even ever ends. But what you do know, is that its full of treasure.

Port Orange

A full and bursting city of climbers, merchants, pirates and preists. The port is built apon rafts and stilts, the city is rickety and hard to navigate, but stands as the center for anyone or thing coming in and out of the tower.

Player Characters

Players will take the role of Climbers, someone who braves the dangers of the unknown Ascian Tower for money, for glory, or for knowledge.
